About Me

Interaction design

I am a UX/UI Designer with front-end skills and experience with mobile & web design. I design interfaces and intuitive interactions for responsive websites, mobile apps and web applications in Figma through motion and subtle interactions.

I have been designing for 5 years and freelancing for 2 years for IT, software and finance companies within agile teams and design systems. LEAN methodology is my way to quickly prototype, test and iterate to find the results that work best for my users.

Design thinking

As a person, I’m a problem solver and enjoy participating in design thinking sprints. In my spare time, I help design students with UX questions and motivate them to be successful designers.
